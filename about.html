About

The person
behind the work.

Industrial designer turned product designer turned founder. I studied industrial & product design at Wentworth, moved into product design across legal tech, wellness, and e-commerce, and somewhere along the way started building the things I design — front end to back end. Off the clock I shoot the landscapes of the American West, run a print shop, and chase the next idea.

How I Work
I design and build.
Front end to back end. Marcavo and Peelr are full products I shipped solo — database, API, and interface. Understanding the whole stack makes every design decision sharper.
Systems, not screens.
I designed both ends of one footwear business — Dardano’s, where you buy your shoes, and EZ Shoe Repair, where you get them fixed — plus design systems spanning iOS and Android.
Design for outcomes.
Revenue, adoption, support load — design isn’t done until it moves the business. I frame decisions in those terms, not just user terms.
