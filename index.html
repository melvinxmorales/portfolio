How I Work
I design and build.
Front end to back end. Marcavo and Peelr are full products I shipped solo — database, API, and interface. Understanding the whole stack makes every design decision sharper.
Systems, not screens.
I designed both ends of one footwear business — Dardano’s, where you buy your shoes, and EZ Shoe Repair, where you get them fixed — plus design systems spanning iOS and Android. I think about how the whole thing connects.
Design for outcomes.
Revenue, adoption, support load — design isn’t done until it moves the business. I frame decisions in those terms, not just user terms.
