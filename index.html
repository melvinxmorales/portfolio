Melvin Morales · Las Vegas, NV

I'm a Senior Product Designer at NetDocuments, where I design enterprise UX for legal professionals using Microsoft Word and the broader NetDocuments platform.

My work sits at the intersection of business strategy and human behavior — translating complex requirements into experiences that are both intuitive and scalable. I bring AI into my workflow as a practical accelerator, not a gimmick.

When I'm not designing, I'm building the Las Vegas UX community through LVUX — a free monthly meetup connecting designers across the valley.
