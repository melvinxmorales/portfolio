How We Got There

01

02

Synthesized research into core insight themes. Defined the design challenge: bring NetDocuments into Word without adding cognitive load or breaking existing compliance workflows.

Designed the ribbon layout, task pane flows, open/save dialogs, version panels, and coauthoring indicators ‚Äî all within Microsoft's strict Office Add-in constraints. Ran moderated usability sessions with legal professionals. Iterated on ribbon layouts to reduce cognitive load, tested open and save flows to minimize required steps, and collaborated with Microsoft engineers to validate technical feasibility.
